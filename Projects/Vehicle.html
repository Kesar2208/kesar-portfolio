Trends Analysis and Sales Prediction for Cars and Two-Wheelers Across Fuel Types

Project Overview

This project provides a comprehensive analysis and forecasting model for the Indian automotive market, specifically focusing on fuel type transitions—Electric Vehicles (EV), CNG, Petrol, and Diesel. Using historical data and ML models, it predicts future trends and policy impact for stakeholders.

Tools Used: Python, Excel
My Role: Data Analyst (Machine Learning)

Objective

The goal was to create a robust forecasting model for fuel-type-based vehicle sales up to 2030 and analyze regional adoption in Maharashtra and Delhi, in response to policy shifts, infrastructure growth, and evolving user preferences.

Dataset Description

  • Vehicle sales stats from: Marklines.com, CEIC Data, GoodCarBadCar.net
  • Government EV policy data from NITI Aayog, state portals
  • Vehicle registration data from Vahan Parivahan
  • Market data from CarDekho, BikeDekho
  • Additional: TATA A (internal/industry data)

Step-by-Step Approach Taken

  1. Data Collection and Integration
  2. ETL (Data Cleaning & Transformation)
  3. Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
  4. Feature Engineering
  5. Model Training (ARIMA, LSTM, XGBoost, Random Forest)
  6. Fuel Station Forecasting (Linear Regression)
  7. Insight Generation
  8. Dashboard Development (Tableau/Power BI)
  9. Recommendations

Key Metrics Tracked

  • Vehicle sales trends (fuel type, region, year)
  • EV/CNG/Petrol/Diesel station growth
  • Market share shifts across fuels
  • Government policy impact on sales
  • Projected crossover points of fuel adoption

    Delhi: EV, CNG, and Petrol/Diesel Stations Forecast: This line chart displays the actual and forecasted growth of various fuel stations in Delhi. It helps in comparing infrastructure development across different states.

    Predicted Sales Crossover Year(Mumbai): A line chart that specifically highlights the forecasted year(s) when sales of EVs or CNG vehicles are projected to overtake traditional petrol/diesel vehicle sales in Mumbai.

    Predicted Sales Crossover Year(Delhi): A line chart that specifically highlights the forecasted year(s) when sales of EVs or CNG vehicles are projected to overtake traditional petrol/diesel vehicle sales in Delhi.

Insights and Final Recommendations

  • Rapid shift towards EV and CNG adoption expected by 2030
  • Traditional fuels projected to decline significantly
  • Policies and infrastructure play critical roles
  • EV/CNG will dominate in key regions like Delhi & Maharashtra

Recommendations

  • Policymakers: Continue subsidies, infrastructure push, clear EV roadmaps
  • Automakers: Expand EV/CNG offerings, invest in R&D and charging infra
  • Consumers: Consider long-term cost and environmental advantages
